This is a Backbone.js demo of a Events Calendar. Click on the 'Details' tab below to see technical details about the application. In the demo, it allows users to view events that are in the calendar. It also allows users who are logged in to create, edit, and delete their own events.

The Events Calendar is coded with the these technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Backbone.js, Underscore.js, FullCalendar plugin, PHP, MySQL, REST, AJAX, JSON, and more.

If you wish to try out this prototype/demo, you must first login with a registered account before you are allowed to create and edit an event. You may also login with the account {username: "demo", password: "demo" }.
